About Me

I'm a data analyst with a passion for turning numbers into narratives that drive smarter business decisions. I’ll be completing my MBA in Business Analytics in May 2025, and I also hold certifications in Google Data Analytics and Tableau Desktop Specialist. With a background in SQL, Excel (PivotTables, VLOOKUP), and Tableau, I’ve worked on projects that range from analyzing R&D spending during COVID-19 to uncovering how evolving NBA playstyles impact TV ratings. I enjoy uncovering patterns, visualizing trends, and presenting insights in ways that are easy to understand and act on. Whether it’s cleaning messy data, building dynamic dashboards, or identifying the “why” behind the numbers, I’m always ready to dive deep and deliver value through data.
